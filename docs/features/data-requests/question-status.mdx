## **Data questions status with Secoda**

There are 4 different statuses for questions, and each question can only have one status. These are:

* Answered

* In review

* In progress

* Unanswered

* Cancelled

![](https://3544070331-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FqxbfBjqa19PllrcxgKxO%2Fuploads%2FdwZNu5UDKrE8jbNaKaWH%2FGroup%20596.png?alt=media&token=8773bd9b-dcbe-419a-937d-bf08f7668987)

Assignees and users can change the status of questions and the assignee will be notified. 

You can also sort all of your questions by status. Simply go to the Questions page, and type in the status of questions that you're looking for.
